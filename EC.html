About

The aim of the project was to come up with a business idea and to develop it. The idea I was working on together with my classmates is about an app named Face2Face, which will help people to spend less time on social media and more time with their friends and family.
The concept is simple. The user should log in to the app, choose a room or create a room that will be joined by his/her friends/family member. Set the time goal and not use the phone during that period of time. If the users can do that, in the end, they gain points that can be redeemed as real rewards from the partner companies.
